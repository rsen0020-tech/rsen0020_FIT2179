Victorian Bushfires

Where, when, and why Victoria burns.

Bushfire is part of life in Victoria, but the risk is not shared evenly. Some communities face fire almost every summer while others rarely do, and the worst seasons are becoming hotter and more destructive. This page maps where fire strikes hardest, traces how it has changed over time, and shows how a warming climate is reshaping the fire season — written for everyday Victorians who want to understand the risk where they live.

Where does Victoria burn?

The map below shades each Local Government Area by the total area burnt. Darker regions carry the heaviest fire load — typically the forested, mountainous east — while cleared agricultural districts burn far less.
